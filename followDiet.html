To do List

  • Wake up early in the morning at 6 am.
  • Go for a walk for at least 1 hour.
  • After walking drink 1 glass of warm water mixed with 1 tsp honey.
  • At 9'o'clock eat home made fruit salad.
  • At 12'o'clock eat proper meal (regular indian cuisine).
  • At 5pm eat some dry fruits and a cup of green tea.
  • At 7 pm eat a meal consisting only of  masala oats.
  • At 8pm walk for at least 1hour.
  • Drink a glass of water after finishing the walk.
