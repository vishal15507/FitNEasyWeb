Stay Hydrated

Getting enough water is very important for your health. Drinking water prevents dehydration ,a condition that might cause unclear thinking resulting in mood change ,causes your body to overheat and lead to constipation and kidney stone. Water helps your body maintain the normal body temperature and lead a healthy life.

Things to keep in mind

    Water Drinking Pattern (1 glass of water)
  • Just after waking up.
  • 30 minutes before breakfast.
  • 1 Hour after breakfast.
  • 30 mins before lunch.
  • 1 hour after lunch.
  • 30 mins after tea break.
  • 30 mins before dinner.
  • Just before going to bed. 
  • Tip: Don't drink all the water at once
